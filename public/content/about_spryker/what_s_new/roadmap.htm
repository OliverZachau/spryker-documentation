Roadmap

Updated: September 3rd 2018

We at Spryker are happy to share our plans with you. Our plans are guidelines that give us direction, that moves us forward to continuously evolve and improve our product. However, we are also flexible and we constantly listen and adapt. Therefore, our plans tend to change. So although we are good fulfilling our commitments, we reserve the right to change our priorities, remove or add new features from time to time. If you are planning anything strategic based on this list, you might want to talk to us first, either by contacting your Spryker representative or one of our Solution Partners. You are also welcome to contribute to the roadmap discussion in our Forum: Feature Roadmap Group.

If you see a feature that you like, send us a mail and let us know why the feature is important to you.

The roadmap contains features, and not architectural items, enhancements, technology updates or any other strategic releases we are working on. We kindly ask you not to base any business decision on these lists without consulting with us first.

Items are listed in no particular order. The order has no relevance to when they will be developed or released:

Features we are working on

Here is the list of features that we are actively working on. You should expect them to be released in next 2-3 months.

Feature Best for Description
Alternative products B2C&B2B When some products are out of stock or discontinued a product manager can identify alternative products that will be offered to customers
Volume prices B2C&B2B Allow shop owner to specify discounted prices based on the quantity of products purchased
Minimum order value B2B A merchant can specify a minimum value of order that can be placed in the shop
Customer specific prices B2B This feature allows defining prices specific for a B2B customer
Product restrictions B2B This feature allows restricting sales of some specific product to a B2B customer
Cart sharing B2B Multiple customers can have access to the same shopping cart with different level of permissions
B2B Company Account B2B Provide your B2B customers with a way to accurately map their business hierarchies, permissions and role management. With the creation of distinctive Business Units, the internal hierarchy can easily be mapped and each Unit can independently operate. A convenient Roles & Permissions System enables your customer's buyers to clearly define purchasing and approval processes.
Continuous development of Front-end API B2C&B2B The API will allow you to build a mobile or one page JS shopping application
Shopping Lists B2B Enable your Business customers to save the products they wish to purchase through the Shopping List Capability. Different roles and permission systems ensure a smooth sharing and contribution management amongst a company's users. Printing a Shopping List, with or without a barcode, make the reordering process easy for your customers. With a direct-to-cart feature, customers can immediately add items to their Cart.
Quick add to cart B2B This feature allows quick add to cart or shopping list of multiple products from one screen
Agent Assist B2C&B2B With the Agent Assistant feature, you can allow your Sales Agents or Customer Care employees to support customers in their purchasing process using your customized storefront. This special view opens your shop as the customer in need of support would see it, but lets your sales personal do the purchase for them. Your company's representative will see all products, prices and discounts as they are for the selected customer. Special discounts can of course still be applied. All purchases done through the Sales Assistant feature will be logged as such.
Measurement Units B2C&B2B Provide ability to sell items in measurements but calculate in items, for example sell in meters but calculate in kilograms.
Packaging Units B2C&B2B Provide ability to sell items in predefined units - blocks, crates, pallets. In inventory they still will be calculated in single items.

Features we plan to work on next

We are currently designing and planning the development of the features below. Longer term roadmap always has a higher level of uncertainty. We are a customer driven company and priorities could shift based on customers' needs and market trends.

Feature Best for Description
Approval Process B2B A workflow that allows a manager to approve purchases made by employees
Split shipment B2C&B2B Ability to deliver an order as several shipments
Personalization framework B2C&B2B A framework for creating customer profile and personalizing customer shopping experience
CMS Enhancements B2C&B2B We are planning a number of CMS enhancements to better support creation and management of marketing static and dynamic content and allow different type of front-end client to access it through external APIs
Quotation and Request for Quote process B2B A quote is used to negotiate with the sales person (e.g. request a better price, more convenient quantities, and additional services). It may be created in response to a request for quote, or as a result of the direct communication with the sales person. Once a customer is happy with the offer in the quote and is ready to proceed with the order, sales person can accept the quote and submit an order.
Merchant Portal B2C&B2B Merchant portal will further extend Marketplace's capabilities of Spryker by offering a user interface to suppliers and merchant to manage their products, stock, prices, and orders
PunchOut B2B A PunchOut Catalog, more accurately called a PunchOut Website, is a method for a corporate purchasing agent to buy from a supplier's website from within the buyer's own procurement application or hosted eprocurement system.
