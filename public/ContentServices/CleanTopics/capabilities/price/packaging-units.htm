ML MS MC Offer cost-efficient and flexible Packaging Units that suit the needs of the product and your customers, such as bags, palettes, or packets in addition to single item packaging. The units can either contain a fixed or variable pre-specified quantity of items. Variable packaging units allow your customers to choose how many individual items they want to purchase, such as a crate of water bottles or 3 individual ones. Easily define restrictions for variable units, such as maximum or minimum item quantities or configure intervals. Single products can be offered in different packaging units, like item, bag, palette or packets. - Packaging units can either have a fixed amount of units or a variable amount - Variable packaging units allow the customer to choose how many individual items he wants within the packaging unit (e.g. a crate of 24 beers but only filled with 10) - If the packaging unit is variable amount restrictions (minimum, maximum and interval) can be configured
