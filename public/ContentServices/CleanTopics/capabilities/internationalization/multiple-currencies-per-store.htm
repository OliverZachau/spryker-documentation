ML MS MC The Spryker Commerce OS lets you to define multiple currencies per store for product, product option and shipping method. A product can for example cost 5 EUR in Germany, 6 EUR in France and 5 CHF in Switzerland. Your customers may easily choose between these different currencies. All prices on the Product Page and in the Cart are adjusted automatically upon changing the currency. Products for which you did not define a price in a specific currency do not appear in the catalog for that currency. - Products for which the price for a currency is not defined do not show in the catalog for that currency
