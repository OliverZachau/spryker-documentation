In the Glossary you can easily store basic text information in different languages so your customers can switch to their prefered one. In the shop, all stored text keys will automatically be adjust, once language is switched. By default, languages are identified by a locale, e.g. de_DE meaning "German language in Germany" and de_CH means "German language in Switzerland".
