Once the discountable items are collected, there are 2 ways to calculate the discounted value. Depending on thecalculator_pluginlinked to the discount, we identify: PLUGIN_CALCULATOR_FIXED- fixed amount discount ( e.g. 10€ off) the currency in which the discount is calculated is the currency configured for the shop PLUGIN_CALCULATOR_PERCENTAGE- percentage discount ( e.g. 10% off) From discount version 5, fixed calculators work with multicurrency and gross/net price modes.
