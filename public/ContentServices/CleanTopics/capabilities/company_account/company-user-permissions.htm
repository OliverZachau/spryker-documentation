"In order to maintain a clear Role and Permission system, you can easily manage which User has what kind of competencies in your shop environment. Any kind of business logic, from simple checks, like signle permissions, to complex ones, purchasing limit, customer allocation and business unit check, can be created to serve your specific needs." A company user may have one or mutiple "Roles". These roles consist of permissions, which are precisely configurable according to the actions the company user with that role is permitted to carry out "- Simple permissions (Does the customer have this permission?) - Complex permissions (Does the customer have this permission including some parameters or business logic?)"
