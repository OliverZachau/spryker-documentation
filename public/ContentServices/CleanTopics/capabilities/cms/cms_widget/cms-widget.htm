With CMS content widgets we are bringing more power to CMS pages and blocks. You can easily include placeholders to display products, product groups and products sets in CMS pages and blocks. Multiple templates allow you to define which template is used per widget. So, you can for example apply different templates to a product widget depending on your use case.
