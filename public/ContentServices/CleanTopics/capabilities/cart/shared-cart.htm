"Enable your business customers to collaborate within their Business Units. By using a Company Account, they can share a cart with other users of the same business unit. Depending on a user's permission, differnet tasks can be perfomed in a shared cart. Each Business Unit user can have either a read or a write permission to the Company Account. With a permission to write, users can perform all cart functionalities, such as checkout. With a permission to read, users have limited options, for example adding items or notes to a cart. " Customers who are users of company accounts can share carts among each other. Each user can have permissions so that for instance there can be users who are allowed to add products to cart and there are other users who can check them out. "- Share a cart with other users of the same business unit - Users of the same business unit can be added to a cart with either a read or a write permission. Write permission allows all cart functionalities whereas read permission only allows the user to add notes to the cart."
