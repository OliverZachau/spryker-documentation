With the Quick Add to Cart option users are enabled to add products via their SKU number to the cart. In a designated field, the SKU and desired quantity can be manually entered to add the item to the cart with one click or directly create an order. For bulk-adding, a defined text area is used to paste a list of product SKUs and quantity. Multiple products can be added to the cart on a single page by selection of SKU and quantity. "- Input of SKU and quantity for multiple products - Copy & paste a list of SKUs to a text area to add them all to cart - Add all items to cart or directly create an order"
