The Spryker Sample Suite is a fully functional, out-of-the-box online shop application. It offers all common functionalities and workflows and can be used as a boilerplate to kick-start your project. With the Dummy Payments feature you are able to perform a sample payment to test the checkout process. Furthermore the Custom Suites offer an opportunity to test a shop application that is tailored to more specific business needs.
